Purpose

My main purspose for us to have this website is that we need to attract many tourist around the world and have many people to view our website and be able to know of our services that we offer. Nowadays many tourist are able to book and pay for services in advance online. This is good for our business to plan ahead and offer good services. On our website our customers will be able to give us a review of our services we give to them and this will attract many customers.

Audience

Paradise River will attract people from 7 years and older. We are in the center of capetown and close to the Table Mountain which is the greatest tourist atraction here in South Africa. We will operate in January, February, March, April, May, October, November and December, during thid month we only close when they is bad weather. Our main target will be families and business companies. We will offer fun and team building activies for small and big group of people. Our audience will access our site with and device they have (laptops, desktop, mobile device) and they can use any device to do their bookings with us . /p>
